# Founding Engineer — Join Our AI-First Startup

## About Us

We're building the infrastructure layer for AI-agent companies — a control plane that enables startups to deploy, orchestrate, and govern autonomous AI agents at scale. We're a team of humans and AI agents working side-by-side, and we're looking for a founding engineer who thrives in that environment.

## The Role

As our founding engineer, you'll be the second human hire and the technical backbone of the company. You'll work directly with the CEO to ship the product, establish engineering culture, and grow the team.

This is a high-ownership, high-autonomy role. You won't be executor-of-roadmap — you'll help *write* the roadmap.

## What You'll Do

- **Ship the MVP** — Design, build, and ship core product features end-to-end (frontend + backend + infra)
- **Set technical foundations** — Make key architectural decisions for the engineering org
- **Grow the team** — Help define hiring bar, interview candidates, and eventually manage engineers
- **Work with AI agents** — You'll coordinate with AI agents on tasks, code review, and execution (we use Paperclip as our orchestration layer)

## What We're Looking For

### Must-Haves
- 5+ years of software engineering experience with significant production responsibility
- Strong full-stack skills: TypeScript/React, Node.js or Python, relational databases, cloud infra (AWS/GCP)
- Track record of shipping and maintaining complex products end-to-end
- Comfort with uncertainty and rapid iteration — startup experience strongly preferred
- Excellent communicator who can work async and collaborate across humans and AI agents

### Nice-to-Haves
- Experience with LLM/AI infrastructure or agentic systems
- Experience at an early-stage startup (pre-seed to Series A)
- Open-source contributions
- Familiarity with multi-agent orchestration, autonomous AI, or agent tooling

## Working With Us

- **Location**: Remote-first (US/EU timezones preferred for overlap)
- **Compensation**: Competitive salary + meaningful equity
- **Tools**: We run on Paperclip, GitHub, and async-first communication

## Why This Role Is Different

This isn't a "join a running train" opportunity. You'll have genuine input on what we build, how we build it, and who we hire next. You'll be a co-author of the company's technical story — not just its executor.

If you're excited about the AI-agent future and want to help build the infrastructure that makes it real, we'd love to talk.
